Classic Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients:

    200g spaghetti
    2 tablespoons olive oil
    3 cloves garlic, minced
    400g canned tomatoes (diced or crushed)
    1 teaspoon salt
    1/2 teaspoon black pepper
    1 teaspoon dried oregano
    1 teaspoon dried basil
    1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
    1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
    Grated Parmesan cheese (for serving)

Instructions:

    Cook Spaghetti:
        Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
        Add the spaghetti and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
        Drain and set aside.

    Prepare Sauce:
        In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
        Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant, about 1 minute.
        Add the canned tomatoes, salt, pepper, oregano, basil, and red pepper flakes (if using).
        Stir well and let the sauce simmer for about 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.

    Combine:
        Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the sauce.
        Toss to coat the spaghetti evenly with the sauce.
        Allow the spaghetti to heat through for a couple of minutes.

    Serve:
        Divide the spaghetti among plates.
        Garnish with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese.
        Serve immediately.
